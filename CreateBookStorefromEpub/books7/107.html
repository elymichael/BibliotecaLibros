A WEEKLY MEETING will be held at Kitchen Place, to teach young
ladies how to cook. Hannah Brown will preside, and all are invited
to attend.

The DUSTPAN SOCIETY will meet on Wednesday next, and parade in
the upper story of the Club House. All members to appear in uniform
and shoulder their brooms at nine precisely.

Mrs. BETH BOUNCER will open her new assortment of Doll's
Millinery next week. The latest Paris fashions have arrived, and
orders are respectfully solicited.

A NEW PLAY will appear at the Barnville Theatre, in the course
of a few weeks, which will surpass anything ever seen on the
American stage. "The Greek Slave, or Constantine the Avenger," is
the name of this thrilling drama!!!

 

HINTS

 

If S.P. didn't use so much soap on his hands, he wouldn't always
be late at breakfast. A.S. is requested not to whistle in the
street. T.T please don't forget Amy's napkin. N.W. must not fret
because his dress has not nine tucks.
