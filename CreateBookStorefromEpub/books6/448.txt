But this just retaliation would not bring Ayrton back to life,
and from the examination of the footprints left in the ground, they
must, alas! conclude that all hopes of ever seeing him again must
be abandoned.

That evening they encamped fourteen miles from Granite House,
and Cyrus Harding calculated that they could not be more than five
miles from Reptile Point.

And indeed, the next day the extremity of the peninsula was
reached, and the whole length of the forest had been traversed; but
there was nothing to indicate the retreat in which the convicts had
taken refuge, nor that, no less secret, which sheltered the
mysterious unknown.
