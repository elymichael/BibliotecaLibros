Chapter

18

 


"Poor man!" said Herbert, who had rushed to the door, but
returned, having seen Ayrton slide down the rope on the lift and
disappear in the darkness.

"He will come back," said Cyrus Harding.

"Come, now, captain," exclaimed Pencroft, "what does that mean?
What! wasn't it Ayrton who threw that bottle into the sea? Who was
it then?"

Certainly, if ever a question was necessary to be made, it was
that one!

"It was he," answered Neb, "only the unhappy man was
half-mad."

"Yes!" said Herbert, "and he was no longer conscious of what he
was doing."
