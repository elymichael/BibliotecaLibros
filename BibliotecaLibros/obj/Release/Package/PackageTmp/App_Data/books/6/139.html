Soon fresher air was felt. The drops of water, dried by
evaporation, no longer sparkled on the walls. The flaring torches
began to grow dim. The one which Neb carried went out, and if they
did not wish to find their way in the dark, they must hasten.

This was done, and a little before four o'clock, at the moment
when the sailor's torch went out in its turn, Cyrus Harding and his
companions passed out of the passage.
