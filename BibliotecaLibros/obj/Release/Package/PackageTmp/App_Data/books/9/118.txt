
"Then Sid he said — he said —"

"I don't think I said anything," said Sid.

"Yes you did, Sid," said Mary.

"Shut your heads and let Tom go on! What did he say, Tom?"

"He said — I think he said he hoped I was better off where I was
gone to, but if I'd been better sometimes —"

"There, d'you hear that! It was his very words!"

"And you shut him up sharp."

"I lay I did! There must 'a' been an angel there. There was an
angel there, somewheres!"

"And Mrs. Harper told about Joe scaring her with a firecracker,
and you told about Peter and the Painkiller —"

"Just as true as I live!"
