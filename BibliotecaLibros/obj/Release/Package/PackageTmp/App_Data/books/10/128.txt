


Chapter

26
Disowned


The King sat musing a few moments, then looked up and said—

"'Tis strange—most strange. &#160;I cannot account for it."

"No, it is not strange, my liege. &#160;I know him, and this
conduct is but natural. &#160;He was a rascal from his birth."

"Oh, I spake not of HIM, Sir Miles."

"Not of him? &#160;Then of what? &#160;What is it that is
strange?"

"That the King is not missed."

"How? &#160;Which? &#160;I doubt I do not understand."
