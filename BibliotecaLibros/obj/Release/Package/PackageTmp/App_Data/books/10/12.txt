
"True, indeed. &#160;I had not thought of that. &#160;I will
consider of her punishment. &#160;Is thy father kind to thee?"

"Not more than Gammer Canty, sir."

"Fathers be alike, mayhap. &#160;Mine hath not a doll's temper.
&#160;He smiteth with a heavy hand, yet spareth me: &#160;he
spareth me not always with his tongue, though, sooth to say.
&#160;How doth thy mother use thee?"

"She is good, sir, and giveth me neither sorrow nor pain of any
sort. And Nan and Bet are like to her in this."

"How old be these?"

"Fifteen, an' it please you, sir."

"The Lady Elizabeth, my sister, is fourteen, and the Lady Jane
Grey, my cousin, is of mine own age, and comely and gracious
withal; but my sister the Lady Mary, with her gloomy mien and—Look
you: &#160;do thy sisters forbid their servants to smile, lest the
sin destroy their souls?"

"They? &#160;Oh, dost think, sir, that THEY have servants?"

The little prince contemplated the little pauper gravely a
moment, then said—

"And prithee, why not? &#160;Who helpeth them undress at night?
&#160;Who attireth them when they rise?"
