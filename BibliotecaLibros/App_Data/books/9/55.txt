"I been to the circus three or four times — lots of times.
Church ain't shucks to a circus. There's things going on at a
circus all the time. I'm going to be a clown in a circus when I
grow up."

"Oh, are you! That will be nice. They're so lovely, all spotted
up."

"Yes, that's so. And they get slathers of money — most a dollar
a day, Ben Rogers says. Say, Becky, was you ever engaged?"

"What's that?"

"Why, engaged to be married."

"No."

"Would you like to?"

"I reckon so. I don't know. What is it like?"

"Like? Why it ain't like anything. You only just tell a boy you
won't ever have anybody but him, ever ever ever, and then you kiss
and that's all. Anybody can do it."

"Kiss? What do you kiss for?"
