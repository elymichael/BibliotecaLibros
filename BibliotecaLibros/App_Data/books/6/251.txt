In the meanwhile the boat-building progressed, and towards the
end of the month half the planking was completed. It could already
be seen that her shape was excellent, and that she would sail
well.

Pencroft worked with unparalleled ardor, and only a sturdy frame
could have borne such fatigue; but his companions were preparing in
secret a reward for his labors, and on the 31st of May he was to
meet with one of the greatest joys of his life.

On that day, after dinner, just as he was about to leave the
table, Pencroft felt a hand on his shoulder.
