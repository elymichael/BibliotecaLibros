Chapter

4

 


"She has blown up!" cried Herbert.

"Yes! blown up, just as if Ayrton had set fire to the powder!"
returned Pencroft, throwing himself into the lift together with Neb
and the lad.

"But what has happened?" asked Gideon Spilett, quite stunned by
this unexpected catastrophe.

"Oh! this time, we shall know—" answered the engineer
quickly.

"What shall we know?—"

"Later! later! Come, Spilett. The main point is that these
pirates have been exterminated!"
