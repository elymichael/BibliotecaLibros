
"Good! now thou'rt wise and reasonable. &#160;And thou'lt
restore the pig?"

"I will, I will indeed—nor ever touch another, though heaven
send it and an archangel fetch it. &#160;Go—I am blind for thy
sake—I see nothing. &#160;I will say thou didst break in and wrest
the prisoner from my hands by force. &#160;It is but a crazy,
ancient door—I will batter it down myself betwixt midnight and the
morning."

"Do it, good soul, no harm will come of it; the judge hath a
loving charity for this poor lad, and will shed no tears and break
no jailer's bones for his escape."
