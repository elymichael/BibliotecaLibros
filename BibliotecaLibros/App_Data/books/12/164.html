"Come, come," said Silver; "stow this talk. He's dead, and he
don't walk, that I know; leastways, he won't walk by day, and you
may lay to that. Care killed a cat. Fetch ahead for the
doubloons."

We started, certainly; but in spite of the hot sun and the
staring daylight, the pirates no longer ran separate and shouting
through the wood, but kept side by side and spoke with bated
breath. The terror of the dead buccaneer had fallen on their
spirits.
