"I wish I could trust myself," said Lord Henry, laughing. "Come,
Mr. Gray, my hansom is outside, and I can drop you at your own
place. Good-bye, Basil. It has been a most interesting
afternoon."

As the door closed behind them, the painter flung himself down
on a sofa, and a look of pain came into his face.
